An example sentence

今日のパリは天気がいいですね。

Kyô-no Pari-wa tenki-ga ii desu-ne.

"The weather in Paris is good today."

Written Japanese

  • Hiragana
    • いいです
    • "ii desu" (is good)
  • Katakana
    • パリ
    • "Pari" (Paris)
  • Kanji
    • 今日
    • "Kyô" (Now-day)
