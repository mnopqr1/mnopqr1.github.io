* An example sentence

 **今日のパリは天気がいいですね。**
 
 Kyô-no Pari-wa tenki-ga ii desu-ne.

 "The weather in Paris is good today."

* Written Japanese

  * /Hiragana/
    + いいです "ii desu" = is good
  * /Katakana/
    + パリ "Pari" = Paris
  * /Kanji/
    + 今日 "Kyô" = Now-day
    + 天気 "Tenki" = Heaven-energy
